## Dataset
- **Source:** Synthetic Sales/Marketing/Operations dataset (500 records)
- **Fields:** OrderID, Date, Region, Category, Product, UnitsSold, UnitPrice, Revenue, CustomerSegment, MarketingSpend, ReturnRate
- **Period:** Jan 2024 – Dec 2024 (12 months)

---

## Key Insights
- **Top Region:** North region contributed 34% of total revenue
- **Best Category:** Electronics led with 42% revenue share
- **Peak Month:** November 2024 had the highest sales (holiday season)
- **Marketing ROI:** Marketing spend had 0.76 correlation with revenue
- **Return Rate:** Operations category had lowest return rate (2.1%)
